Cornflake Biscuits

Cook Time: 25 mins

Ingredients

250g butter

1/2 cup sugar

1 egg

2 teaspoons golden syrup

2 cups plain flour

1 tablespoon baking powder

1 teaspoon cream of tartar

1 teaspoon bicarb of soda

2 cups sultanas (or walknuts and chopped dates)

a bowl of cornflakes

Directions

Cream butter and sugar. Add egg and syrup. Sift dry ingredients, then add sultanas. Put cornflakes in a basin, drop in a teaspoon of the mixture and roll in flakes. Bake in slow oven at 180 degress celsuis for 25 minutes.
