Shrimp Dumplings

★★★★★

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

Filling:

400g peeled shrimp

100g pork fat

100g Bamboo shoots


Seasoning:

1 tsp Salt

1tsp Sugar

1/2 tsp Chicken Stock Powder

A dash of pepper

1/2 Egg white

2 tsp Potato Starch

1 tsp Sesame Oil


Pastry:

200g Gluten-free Wheat Flour

60g Potato Starch

380g Boiling Water

1/2 tsp Lard

Directions

Wash and drain shrimp.
Boil pork fat and finely dice.
Boil bamboo shoots.
Dry all the ingredients well to remove most of the moisture.
Mix shrimp and bamboo shoots till it becomes sticky.
Add seasoning and and fat and mix well.
Set aside in fridge for at least 30 minutes.

Sift the Wheat flour and Potato Starch in a bowl and pour in hot water. Stir well.
Knead the dough for a good 3-4 minutes. Add Lard and continue to knead.
Roll the dough into a long piece and cut into smaller pieces.
Press with a cleaver to make it very thin.

Add mixture to the dumpling skins and arrange in a steamer. It is a good idea to use baking paper or something else so they don't stick.

Cook in the steamer for about 6 minutes.

Notes

When mixing ingredients, always stir in one direction
