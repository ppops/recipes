Sticky Date Pudding

Dessert

Difficulty: Easy Source: Louise Amato

Ingredients

200g chopped dates

1 and 1/4 cups of water

1 teaspoon bicarb of soda

3/4 cup brown sugar

2 eggs

1 cup self-raising flour


Sauce:

1/4 cup brown sugar

1/2 cup cream

100g butter

Directions

Bring water to boil and add chopped dates and bicarb of soda, let stand for 5 minutes.
In a separate bowl, put cream, sugar and butter. Add one egg at a time.
Gently fold in the sifted flour and add the date mixture.
Pour into a 20cm round greased cake tin.
Bake for 55 minutes at 180 degrees.
Let it cool and then put onto serving dish. Pour warm sauce over it and serve,
Sauce: combine all ingredients in a saucepan, bring to boil until smooth.

Enjoy!!!!!
