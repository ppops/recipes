Shamy's Roast Vegetable & Barley Soup

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6 Source: Shaun & Amy

Ingredients

2 litres of chicken stock

1 cup of barley

1 chunk of pumpkin

3 potatoes

2 zucchini

2 carrots

2 onions

1 sweet potato

5 cloves of garlic

Salt and pepper

Olive oil

Directions

Preheat oven at 220 degrees.
Bring stock to boil and add the barley. Let simmer for 35 mins.
Chop all veggies and place on 2-3 baking trays.
Drizzle with olive oil until all veggies are coated.
Place veggies in the oven for 30-40 mins at 220 degrees.
Add cooked veggies to stock and blend.
Add salt and pepper and serve with crusty bread.
