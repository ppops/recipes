Green Curry Paste

Ingredients

½ tsp coriander seeds

¼ tsp cumin seeds

6 white peppercorns


1 good pinch of salt

1tsp fresh garlic, finely chopped

1tsp fresh ginger, finely chopped

4 coriander roots, roughly chopped

1 tbsp galangal, finely chopped

¼ tsp ground tumeric


1 lemongrass stem (pale part only), chopped (keep upper pieces for curry)

2 long green chilie, roughly chopped

4 small green chilis, roughly chopped (or an extra 2 long green chilis)

4 kaffir lime leaves, spine removed, roughly chopped

2 small Asian red shallots, roughly chopped

Directions

Gently toast coriander, cumin and peppercorns in a small frypan to release flavours, taking care not to burn.
Place cooked spices in a mortar & pestle and grind to a powder.
Add the ingredients, from salt to tumeric, into the mortar & pestle (or use a stick blender) and process until they break down.
Add the rest of the ingredients gradually (so its easier to work with) and continue grinding till a paste is formed.
