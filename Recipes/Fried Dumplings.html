Fried Dumplings

★★★★★

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

Dumpling skins


Filling:

250g Pork or Chicken mince

100g Prawn meat

2 big pieces of Chinese cabbage

50g chopped Chives

1/2 tbsp chopped Green onion (white part only)

1/2 tbsp chopped Ginger


Seasoning:

1 tsp Salt

1 tbsp Sugar

1/2 tsp Chicken Stock Powder

1 tsp Sesame Oil

1 tbsp Potato Starch

1 small Egg

50g Chicken Stock


Dipping Sauce:

1 tbsp Hot Water

1 tbsp Sugar

1 tbsp Black Vinegar (Chinkiang Vinegar)

1 tbsp Light Soy Sauce

A piece of ginger cut in long thin pieces


Paste for frying:

230g water

10g wheat flour




If you want to make your own pastry:


250g Plain Flour

100g Boiling Water

65g Cold Water

Directions

Wash prawns in Plain Flour and drain. Then smash with a tenderizer or chop finely.
Add a big pinch of Salt to the Chinese Cabbage. Leave for 5 minutes and then drain by squeezing with hands.
Mix meat with prawns till combined and mixture is sticky.
Add vegetables till combined.
Add seasoning till combined.
Add chicken stock slowly while mixing.
Set aside in fridge for at least 30 minutes.

Make dumpling by placing a spoonful of the mixture into the dumpling skin and folding over.
Arrange dumpling laying upwards in a large saucepan and pour Paste around the dumplings.

Once the colour changes, add some vegetable oil around the dumplings and continue cooking for a few minutes.

Flip it over to serve with pancake-like film on top.



If you want to make your own pastry:

Add boiling water into flour, mix evenly then add cold water to make a fine dough. Rest the dough for 30 mins. Divide the dough into 35 small pieces.

Notes

When mixing ingredients, always stir in one direction.
