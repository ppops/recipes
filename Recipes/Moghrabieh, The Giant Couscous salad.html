Moghrabieh, The Giant Couscous salad

Prep Time: 5 Min Prep Cook Time: 20 Min Cook Source: Lifestylefood.com.au

Ingredients

250g Dried Moghrabieh

500ml Chicken Stock or Water

1 Cinnamon Quill

3 Cloves

1 Star Anise

1 Small Red Chilli

2 Ripe Tomatoes

1 Red Onion

1 Lemon

100ml Red Wine Vinegar

1/2 Bunch Continental Parsley

50g Sultanans

100g Fetta (Crumbled)

1 handful Baby Spinach

2 Tblsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Salt & Pepper

Directions

1. Soak the sultanas in the red wine vinegar.

2. Combine the chicken stock or water in a pan with the cinnamon quill, cloves and star anise. Bring to the boil and add the moghrabieh. Bring back to the boil then simmer for 15-20 minutes or until just soft.

3. Meanwhile finely dice the tomatoes, red onion and chilli. Zest and juice the lemon and chop the parsley.

4. Once the moghrabieh is cooked, strain and discard the cinnamon, cloves and star anise but retain some of the liquid.

5. Add the tomato, onion, chilli, lemon juice & zest. Now add the sultanas including the vinegar they were soaked in. Stir through the baby spinach, the remaining heat will wilt it. Crumble the fetta and season with salt & pepper and add the olive oil and parsley.

6. If the mixture seems a little dry, add some of the retained cooking liquid.

7. This keeps well in a fridge for afew days and can be rewarmed so don't worry if you make too much!
