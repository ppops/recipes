Warak Enab

★★★★★

Difficulty: Medium Source: Liv's auntie

Ingredients

Vines Leaves


Stuffing:

3 cups of washed medium grain rice

500g minced beef

3/4 finely diced onion

1 teaspoon of pepper

2 pinches of salt

1/2 grated whole nutmeg

4 teaspoons allspice pimento

60g melted butter

2 tablespoons of oil


Additional ingredients for cooking:

Garlic

Nutmeg

Allspice

Salt

Pepper

Lemon

Directions

In hot water, wash the leaves thoroughly and soak for 1.5 hours. Additional blanching of the leaves may be required if the leaves aren't soft enough.

Combine the stuffing contents in a big bowl.
Roll the stuffing in the vines leaves.

Stack the Warak Enabs in a big saucepan (rows on top of rows).
On top on each row, add the additional ingredients.
Bring to the boil and then simmer for 30-40 minutes.

Add lemon on top when serving.


Note: You can store the uncooked rolled Warak Enabs in the freezer.
