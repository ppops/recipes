Sultana and Nut Muffins

Dessert

Prep Time: 15 minutes or less Servings: 6 Source: Bestrecipes.com.au

Ingredients

1 cup LSA mix

1 cup shredded coconut

½ cup sugar

½ cup sultanas

¼ cup orange juice, or juice of choice

¼ cup canola oil

2 large eggs

1 teaspoon mixed spice

1 teaspoon gluten-free baking powder or bicarbonate of soda

1 teaspoon vanilla essence (optional)

Chocolate (optional)

Banana (optional)

Nuts (optional)

Directions

Preheat oven to 180°C, and spray a 6 hole muffin tin with canola oil.
Mix all ingredients thoroughly with a wooden spoon.
Divide mixture between muffin holes.
Bake for 20-30 minutes or until browned.
Allow to cool slightly, then carefully remove from pan.
Serve topped with sugar-free jam, if desired.
