Kimchi Soup

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Servings: 4 Source: My Collection

Ingredients

200-250g Kimchi

1 cup water

3 cups broth or stock

300-400 g sliced pork (any meat is fine, really)

tofu (silken(slice into large chunks and gently put into pot) or firm( cubed), whichever you like)

egg, hardboiled and peeled

garlic, minced

noodles (any kind of noodles are okay)


Optional


2 thick slices ginger

1 onion, thinly sliced

2 garlic cloves, sliced

3 star anise

2 cinnamon quills, lightly bruised

2 tbs caster sugar

1/3 cup (80ml) fish sauce

1 cup (80g) bean sprouts

1 long red chilli, seeds removed, thinly sliced

1/3 cup each of basil, mint and coriander leaves, to serve

Sriracha chili sauce

Directions

Stir fry the meat with a little oil and garlic until cooked.

Add water, stock, and kimchi. Bring to a boil, stir occasionally.

Add tofu, noodles and egg. Bring to a boil again on medium heat.

If it isnâ€™t spicy enough, you can add Sriracha chili sauce.

Add whatever you like from the optional ingredients.

The longer it cooks, the better it tastes.
