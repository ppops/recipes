Bean chilli

Prep Time: 0:5 Cook Time: 0:15 Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: Taste.com.au

Ingredients

2 tablespoons Cobram Estate Extra Virgin Olive Oil

1 onion, chopped

2 cloves garlic, crushed

1 red capsicum, chopped

1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning

1 small fresh red chilli, chopped

400g can Coles Brand 4 bean mix

420g can Mexican chilli beans

400g can diced tomatoes

2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley

450g pkt Sunrice microwave white rice

1/4 cup natural yoghurt, to serve

Select all ingredients

Directions

Step 1

Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and cook for 4-5 mins or until soft. Add the garlic and cook for 30 secs. Add capsicum, Cajun seasoning, chilli, 4 bean mix, Mexican chilli beans and tomatoes and cook for 8-10 mins or until thickened. Stir in parsley.

Step 2

Cook the rice following packet directions. Serve the chilli with rice and yoghurt.
