Mussels steamed with tomato and garlic

Prep Time: Preparation time: 10 minutes Cook Time: Cooking time: 10 minutes Servings: Recipe highlights Serves: 4 Good source of iron Best served immediately Dairy free Gluten free Egg free Nut free Source: Betterhealth.vic.gov.au

Ingredients

1 tablespoon olive oil

4 cloves garlic, crushed

1/2 cup dry white wine

pinch saffron threads

1 kg mussels, shells scrubbed and beards removed (make sure you buy mussels with their shells closed and throw out any that do not open during cooking)

500 g ripe red tomatoes, skins removed and chopped (this can be done by placing raw tomatoes in a saucepan of boiling water for 1 minute, then remove, allow to cool slightly and then remove skins)

1 tablespoon parsley, chopped

1 tablespoon basil, shredded

black pepper, freshly ground

Directions

1. Heat oil in a wok or large frying pan over high heat.
2. Add the garlic and cook briefly until fragrant (make sure not to burn the garlic).
3. Add the wine and saffron. Bring the mixture to the boil and add the mussels. Cover with a lid and cook for 3-4 minutes.
4. Remove the lid, shake the pan and remove cooked mussels as they open.
5. Keep warm until most are opened and throw out any unopened mussels.
6. Add the tomatoes to the pan along with the parsley and basil.
7. Season to taste.

Notes

Notes: Serve mussels in deep bowls and pour the tomato mixture over the top - can be served with your favourite crusty bread or pasta.
