Honey soy chicken drumsticks

Prep Time: 0:10 Cook Time: 0:45 Difficulty: Easy Servings: 12 Source: Taste.com.au

Ingredients

125mls (1/2 cup) light soy sauce

80mls (1/3 cup) honey

1 lemon, juiced

1 garlic clove, crushed

12 chicken drumsticks

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 180°C.

Step 2

Combine soy sauce, honey, lemon juice and garlic in a bowl and mix well. Place the chicken drumsticks in an ovenproof dish in a single layer and pour the marinade over the top.

Step 3

Cover the dish with foil and cook drumsticks in preheated oven for 45 minutes or until the juices run clear when the drumsticks are pierced in the thickest part. Turn the drumsticks and baste them with the marinade every 15 minutes. Remove from oven, drain off marinade and cool to room temperature.

Step 4

Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 days. If you want to reheat the drumsticks, preheat the oven to 140ÂºC. Place the drumsticks in an ovenproof dish and reheat them for 10 minutes or until heated through.
