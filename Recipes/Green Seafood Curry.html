Green Seafood Curry

Ingredients

1 red onion, finely chopped

2 clove garlic, finely chopped

1-2 tbsp Green Curry Paste (according to taste)

200ml coconut milk

1-2 red chilis, chopped or whole (deseed for milder heat)

1-2 tbsp fish sauce

1 tbsp sugar

2 tbsp vegetable oil

600g mixed cleaned seafood (can be more or less) (prawns, fish fillets, mussels)

2 tbsp basil leaves

1 good pinch salt

1 good pinch pepper

1 lemon or juice, juiced

Directions

Heat oil in frypan, add the onions and garlic and fry gently for a few minutes until they soften.
Add Green Curry Paste and mix, simmering for a couple of minutes to release the flavours.
Add cocnut milk, chili, fish sauce, sugar and simmer for 5 minutes.
Mix, then gently arrange seafood into the curry, adding larger pieces of fish first. Add half the basil leaves.
Spoon a little of the liquid over the seafood.
Simmer gently 8-10 minutes until seafood is cooked. Cover with foil to keep heat in.
Taste sauce for seasoning adding salt and pepper to taste.
Mix the citris juice through the rich sauce.
Carefully remove seafood onto setving platter.
Ladle the curry sauce over the seafood and top with the remaining basil leaves to serve.
