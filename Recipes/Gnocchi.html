Gnocchi

Prep Time: 2 hrs Cook Time: 2 hrs Difficulty: Medium Source: Paprikaapp.com

Ingredients

Sauce

400-500 grams of Scotch fillet

1 onion

2 cloves of garlic

2 tsps sugar

Herbs for flavour


Gnocchi

200 grams plain flour (+ extra to add if dough too wet)

Pinch of salt

1 egg

1kg Desiree potatoes (medium sized for quicker boiling)

Directions

Meat Ragu Sauce for Gnocchi
1. Brown onion in a large pan
2. Once onion is soft and brown, add garlic for quick browning
3. Add diced scotch fillet
4. Once meat is brown all over, add sauce, sugar and herbs
5. Let simmer for 2 hours until meat is tender and falls apart.

Gnocchi
1. Boil potatoes with skin for a long time. Check with a knife to see if soft inside, but not too soft.
2. Remove skins and crush potatoes in metal crusher.
3. Let crushed potatoes cool on a clean surface.
4. Make a well in the middle of potatoes and crack the egg in the middle. Add salt.
5. Mix together with hands and add flour to bring together.
DO NOT OVERWORK THE MIXTURE.
6. Roll into a long sausage once the consistency is right.
7. Cut into small pieces and roll each piece lightly onto a fork with your finger.
8. Add into a deep pot of boiling salty water. When they rise to the top they are ready.
9. Rinse with cold water once they have been taken out of the boiling water.
10. Add a little bit of sauce so that the gnocchi doesn't stick together.
Serve and ENJOY!
