Siu Mai (Steamed Pork Dumplings)

★★★★

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

200g Lean Pork

200g Pork Fat

250g Prawns

About 500g Wonton pastry sheets


Seasoning:

1/2 tsp Salt

1 tsp Sugar

1 tsp Chinese (Shao Xing) cooking wine

A dash of pepper

1 tsp Sesame Oil

1/2 tbsp Osyter sauce

1/2 tbsp Soy Sauce

1 small Egg white

1 tbsp Potato Starch

1/2 tsp Chicken Stock Powder

Directions

Wash and wipe dry Prawns with kitchen paper tower then smash with tenderizer or chop finely.
Wash Pork and Pork Fat and dry. Cut into small pieces till it's almost minced.
Mix Pork till mixture is sticky. Then add Prawns and seasoning.
Set aside in fridge for at least 30 minutes.

Put filling in Wonton sheets and wrap tightly. Leave the top exposed.

Steam for about 7 minutes till meat is fully cooked.

Garnish dumplings with cavier.

Notes

When mixing ingredients, always stir in one direction.
