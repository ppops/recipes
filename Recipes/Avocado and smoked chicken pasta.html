Avocado and smoked chicken pasta

★★★★

Dinner

Cook Time: Time to make:  20 mins Difficulty: Easy-Medium Servings: Serves: 4 Source: Healthyfood.co.nz

Ingredients

350g spiral pasta

200g broccoli, cut into small florets

400g smoked chicken breast, skin removed and roughly chopped

1 avocado, roughly chopped

4 tomatoes, quartered, deseeded and thinly sliced

1/4 cup finely chopped fresh basil

1/2 cup low-fat plain yoghurt

2 tablespoons low-fat mayonnaise

Directions

Step 1 Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling water for 9 minutes. Add the broccoli and cook for a further 2 minutes. Drain and place in a large bowl.

Step 2 Add the chicken, avocado and tomatoes to the pasta.

Step 3 To make the dressing, place the basil, yoghurt and mayonnaise in a small bowl and stir to combine. Add to the pasta and toss gently to combine. Serve this dish warm or cold as a salad.

Notes

HFG Tip
Slice and add the avocado just before serving if making this dish ahead.
