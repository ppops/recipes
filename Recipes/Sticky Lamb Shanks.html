Sticky Lamb Shanks

★★★★★

Dinner

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 3 hrs Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4 Source: Electrolux Cooking School

Ingredients

4 lamb shanks - cut French style

1 ½ tbsp olive oil

1 medium onion, cut into eigths

3 carrots, cut into 1" chunk

2 celery stalks, cut into ¼" pieces

1 clove garlic chopped

1 tbsp tomato paste

½ tbsp chopped basil

½ tbsp chopped mint

½ tbsp rosemary pulled off stem

3 tbsp ABC sweet soy sauce

3 tbsp sweet chilli sauce

1 cup red wine

500-700ml chicken stock

flour

salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Preheat oven to 180° (old oven) or 160° (new oven).

Roll shanks in flour to fully cover and season with salt and pepper.

Heat olive oil in pot. Add lamb shanks and brown all sides including the ends. Remove to a plate.

Reduce heat to medium and add onions, carrots and celery to the pot. Cook, stirring occasionally until all ingredients begin to brown.

Add tomato paste, sauces, herbs and garlic. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently. Add red wine.

Return shanks to pot and add enough chicken stock to come half way up the sides of the shanks.

Cover the pot and place in the centre of the oven. Cook for 3 hours, check every hour and turn shanks.

Serve with mash potatoes and green beans.
