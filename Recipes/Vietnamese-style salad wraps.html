Vietnamese-style salad wraps

Prep Time: 0 hr 0 min Cook Time: 0 hr 0 min Servings: 1 serving Source: My Collection

Ingredients

Iceberg lettuce

Cooked and cooled rice vermicelli

Pickled carrot

Cucumber

Mint

Poached chicken (infused with ginger, garlic, spring onions and black peppercorns)


Nuoc Cham sauce (a sweet/tangy blend of fish sauce, rice vinegar, lime juice and sugar)

Directions

Combine ingredients in lettuce and dip in sauce.
