Horchata

Prep Time: 10 min Servings: 6 Source: M.allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 1/3 cup uncooked white long-grain rice

5 cups water

1 cup milk

1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract

2 cinnamon sticks

Ground cinnamon

1/3 cup white sugar

Directions

Pour the rice, cinnamon sticks and 2 cups of water into the blender; blend until the rice and cinnamon are roughly ground, about 1 minute.

Pour into a pitcher and place in the refrigerator to soak (overnight is best) - or at the very least 4-5 hours.

Strain the rice water into a pitcher and discard the rice. Stir the milk, vanilla and sugar into the rice water. Chill and stir before serving over ice with cinnamon sprinkled on top.
