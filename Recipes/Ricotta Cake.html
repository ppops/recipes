Ricotta Cake

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 45 mins Servings: 8

Ingredients

3 eggs

100g butter melted

300g self raising flour

300g caster sugar

1 tub of ricotta cheese

icing sugar

Directions

Preheat oven at 180 degrees.

Combine all ingredients into a bowl and mix with an electric mixer.

Sprinkle icing sugar on cake once cooled.
