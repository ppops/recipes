Shaun's Sushi

Prep Time: 1 hr Cook Time: 30 mins Difficulty: Medium Servings: 1 serving Source: Shaun Hidge

Ingredients

2 cups sushi rice (short or medium grain)

3 cups water

1/2 cup (125ml) Japanese distiller rice vinegar

1/4 cup white sugar

1 tsp salt

Nori sheets

1/3 cup of mayo with 1 tsp wasabi

1 Lebanese cucumber, seeded and cut into long strips

180 grams canned tuna

Japanese soy sauce for dipping

Directions

1. Rinse 2 cups rice and place in saucepan with 3 cups water and bring to boil.
2. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 12 mins.
3. After 12 mins remove from heat, keep covered and let stand for 15 mins.
4. Meanwhile make the rice seasoning: rive vinegar, sugar and salt
5. Heat seasoning mix gently stirring occasionally until sugar dissolves then let cool.
6. Tip rice into large tray and add seasoning
7. Chop rice with spatula and let cool.

Once cooled
8. Use sushi express to make sushi
9. Place nori sheet in glossy side down and fill express maker
