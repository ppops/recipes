Apple Strudel

★★★★★

Dessert

Prep Time: 1 hr Cook Time: 35 mins Difficulty: Hard Servings: 1 strudel Source: Nonna

Ingredients

Sultana Mix:

100g Sultanas

1/3 cup Dark Rum

1/3 cup White Wine

2 tablespoons Sugar


Apple Mix:

100g Butter

100g Breadcrumbs

400g Apples from tin

1 teaspoon Cinnamon

2 tablespoons Drinking Chocolate

2 tablespoons Sugar

2 Teddy Bear Biscuits


Pastry:

200g Plain Flour

200g Self-raising Flour

1/2 teaspoon Salt

Rind of 1 Lemon

2 tablespoons Sugar

2 Eggs

1 tablespoon Dark Rum

2 tablespoons Olive Oil

1/2 Teaspoon Baking Powder

Warm Milk

1 Egg (for brushing pastry)

Directions

- Prepare Sultana Mix the night before. Combine ingredients in a glass and leave overnight. (See
Image 1)
- Melt Butter in a pan.
- Brown the breadcrumbs.
- Add Apples, Cinnamon, Drinking Chocolate, Sugar. Mix for approx. one minute.
- Add Crushed Teddy Bear Biscuits and Sultana Mix. Mix for approx. 2 minutes. Set aside. (See Image 2)
- Make a well in the flour. Add Salt, Lemon Rind, Sugar, Eggs, Dark Rum, Olive Oil and Baking
Powder. Knead mixture well until very smooth. Add the Warm Milk if necessary.
- Heat up a very hot saucepan and place dough underneath for 5-10 mins ensuring no air is
leaked. (See Image 3)
- Roll out dough till very thin and even, into a round shape.
- Spread the Apple mix evenly over the dough. (See Image 5 and 6)
- Start rolling the dough. With every roll be sure to poke holes with a fork in the pastry. (See Image
7)
- Make into a C shape and fold corners together.
- Brush pastry with Egg to achieve a shiny finish.

Cook on 180°C for 25-35 mins.
