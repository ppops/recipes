Beef Satay Skewers with Couscous

Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: 10 min Source: Taste.com.au

Ingredients

500g diced rump steak (3cm cubes)

250g cherry tomatoes

1 yellow capsicum, cut into 3cm pieces

250ml satay sauce

Olive oil spray

200g (1 cup) wholemeal couscous

Coriander leaves, to serve

Baby salad leaves, to serve

Directions

Using 8 metal or bamboo skewers, thread the beef, tomatoes and capsicum alternatively onto the skewers. Season with sea salt and pepper.

Brush the skewers with half the satay sauce, reserving the remaining sauce to serve.

Pre-heat a lightly oiled barbecue or char-grill pan over medium-high heat and cook skewers for 8 to 10 minutes, turning often, or until lightly charred and cooked through.

Meanwhile, prepare the couscous according to the packet instructions.

Serve the skewers drizzled with the remaining satay sauce, couscous sprinkled with coriander and baby salad leaves on the side.
