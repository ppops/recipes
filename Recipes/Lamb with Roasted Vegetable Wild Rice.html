Lamb with Roasted Vegetable Wild Rice

Prep Time: 0:10 Cook Time: 0:25 Difficulty: Easy Servings: Servings 4 Source: Taste.com.au

Ingredients

1 red onion, cut into thin wedges

1 red capsicum, coarsely chopped

450g (about 1/2) cauliflower, cut into florets

1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil

1/2 teaspoon paprika

1 teaspoon ground cumin

12 Coles Australian Lamb Cutlets

250g pkt Tilda Brown Basmati & Wild Rice

1/3 cup (35g) walnuts, toasted

30g baby rocket leaves

1/2 cup (140g) Greek-style yoghurt

2 tablespoons tahini

2 tablespoons lemon juice

1 garlic clove, crushed

Select all ingredients

Directions

Preheat oven to 220C. Line a baking tray with baking paper. Combine onion, capsicum and cauliflower in a bowl. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of the oil. Add the paprika and cumin. Season. Toss to coat. Transfer to prepared tray. Roast for 20 mins or until vegetables are just tender and lightly browned.

Meanwhile, to make the tahini sauce, combine the yoghurt, tahini, lemon juice and garlic in a small bowl. Season.

Heat remaining oil in a frying pan or chargrill pan. Cook lamb for 2 mins each side or until cooked to your liking. Transfer to a plate and cover with foil.

Heat the rice following packet directions. Place in a large bowl with roasted vegetables, walnuts and rocket. Toss to combine. Serve immediately with lamb and tahini sauce.
