Chocolate and Almond Cake from Positano

Dessert

Prep Time: 0 hr 0 min Cook Time: 0 hr 0 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 1 serving Source: My Collection

Ingredients

200 g 64-70 o/o fine dark chocolate (valhrona)

200 g Unsalted butter

200 g Caster sugar

5 eggs (Separated)

250 g Peeled blanched almonds (pulsed to fine meal)

Finely grated lemon zest of 1 lemon

Icing sugar for dusting

Directions

Preheat oven to 180c.
Butter a 23cm diameter loose bottom tin and lined with paper.
Break the chocolate into small pieces, place in a bowl over simmering water and heat until melted.
Add the butter in pieces and gently stir in
Add the sugar and stir to a creamy consistency.
Remove from heat and cool slightly.
Add the egg yolks.
Stir in the almonds.
In a large bowl whisk the egg whites until soft peaks form, not too dry or the cake will have a hard crust.
Add the lemon zest.
Gently fold in the chocolate, ensuring you don't over mix.
Pour the mixture into the tin and bake for 30-40 mins. Until the cake is a lovely dark brown,has a slight crust and is shrinking away from the sides of the tin.
When cool turn out onto wire rack and dust with icing sugar.
Serve with mascarpone with Grand Marnier mixed in to taste.
