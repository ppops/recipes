Healthy Banana Bread

Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2 Source: Taste.com.au

Ingredients

8 tablespoons Caster sugar

370 gr. Ripe banana, mashed

½ cup Vegetable oil

2 Eggs

270 gr. Plain flour, sifted

2 teaspoons Vanilla essence

1 teaspoon Baking soda

½ teaspoon Baking powder

½ teaspoon Table salt

60 gr. Raw almond, coarsely chopped

Directions

In a mixing bowl add all the dry ingredients. Then mix in the wet ingredients with a wooden spoon. Be careful not to over stir. Just fold the dry ingredients in until it’s roughly mixed, this will keep the bread crumbly and fluffy.

Pour the bread batter in a greased cake pan.

Bake at 170 degrees Celcius in a preheated fan forced oven for approximately 40-50 minutes or until golden brown.

Leave to cool for about 5 minutes before tipping out onto a cooling rack. It is fantastic on its own, but I sometimes spread butter on mine :)
