Red Beef Curry

★★★★★

Main meals

Prep Time: 25 min Cook Time: 1 hr 45 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: Mum

Ingredients

2 tablespoons Peanut Oil

1 medium brown onion (chopped)

1kg diced chuck steak

1 crushed clove of garlic

2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger

1 tablespoon thinly sliced lemongrass

1 cup beef stock

2 tablespoons red curry paste

1/2 cup water

400ml can of coconut milk

1 Tablespoon lime juice

1 teaspoon fish sauce

1/2 cup coconut cream

1/4 cup roughly chopped fresh coriander leaves

Directions

Heat half the oil in a large heavy-based pan and cook the steak, in batches, until browned.

Heat the remaining oil in the same pan, cook the onion, garlic, lemongrass, ginger and paste, stirring, until the onion is soft.

Return the steak to the pan with the stock, water and coconut milk.

Bring to the boil then simmer, covered, for 1 hour.

Remove the lid, simmer, uncovered, for about 45 minutes or until steak is tender.

Stir in the juice, fish sauce, coconut cream and coriander.

Cook until hot.

Serve with steamed rice.


Note: Also suitable for freezing.
